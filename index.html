Intro to Python Programming

What is it?

This "course" will introduce students to programming using Python. It will cover the fundamentals of programming and equip students with the skills neccesary to begin learning computer science in most programming langauges. The course consists of lecture-slideshows, mini-assignments, and a large project. **NONE of the assignments are checked and are only meant for the students**

Prerequisites & Resources used

We ask that students are in the higher middle school (or early high school) grades and are literate with computers. We'll be using Google Docs, Google Slides, Google Forms, and the Replit IDE. We also ask that students come WITH laptops (if the student doens't own a personal laptop a school-chromebook will suffice)
